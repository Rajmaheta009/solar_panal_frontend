  • THREE PHASE INVERTER

Grace is a well known name in several industries for providing innovatory three phase DC to AC power inverter that combines the MMPT (Maximum Power Point Tracker) technology in conjunction with efficient power conversion configuration. The MMPT helps to max-out the available energy from the strings of solar panels at any time during its operation. Additionally, a team of Grace has addressed fixed voltage technology to ensure continues working of the inverter at its best possible input voltage, in spite of Inclement weather conditions. This inverter is available in different voltage configuration, equipped with temperature LED indicator and overload LED indicator for monitoring. It is efficient and economical solution for commercial and industrial systems for DC to AC power conversion, Three Phase Inverter is used where the large amount of DC to AC conversion is required.
